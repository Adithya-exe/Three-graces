THE

THREE GRACES

The Three Graces, known as the Charites in Greek mythology, symbolize beauty, charm, and joy. Traditionally named Aglaea (Splendor), Euphrosyne (Mirth), and Thalia (Good Cheer), they embody grace, elegance, and artistic inspiration. Often depicted in classical art, they represent unity, generosity, and timeless beauty.
